## How to Log In

### Step 1: Go to the Login Page
Navigate to the Diligince.ai login page at your organization's URL.

### Step 2: Enter Your Email
1. Enter your registered email address
2. Click "Continue" or "Find Accounts"
3. The system will search for accounts linked to your email

### Step 3: Select Your Account
If you have multiple accounts (e.g., Industry and Vendor):
1. A list of available accounts will appear
2. Each shows: Role, Company Name, Last Login
3. Click on the account you want to access

### Step 4: Enter Password
1. Enter your password
2. Click "Sign In"

### Step 5: Two-Factor Authentication (If Enabled)
If 2FA is enabled for your account:
1. You'll receive a 6-digit code via email/SMS
2. Enter the code
3. Click "Verify"

### Login Troubleshooting

**Problem: "Account not found"**
- Check if you're using the correct email
- Contact your Industry Admin to verify your account

**Problem: "Email not verified"**
- Check your email for verification link
- Click "Resend Verification" if needed

**Problem: "Phone not verified"**
- Complete phone verification first
- Enter the OTP sent to your phone

**Problem: "Forgot password"**
- Click "Forgot Password" link
- Enter your email
- Follow the reset instructions sent to your email

## Understanding the Navigation

### Main Navigation Areas

#### 1. Sidebar (Left)
The sidebar contains all main modules:
- **Dashboard** - Overview and quick stats
- **Requirements** - Procurement requirements
- **Quotations** - Vendor quotes
- **Purchase Orders** - PO management
- **Project Workflows** - Project tracking
- **Messages** - Communication
- **Analytics** - Reports
- **Diligince HUB** - Vendor discovery
- **Settings** - Configuration

#### 2. Header (Top)
- Company logo and name
- Search bar
- Notifications bell
- User profile menu

#### 3. Main Content Area (Center)
- Displays the current page content
- Tables, forms, charts, etc.

### Navigation Tips

- **Expand/Collapse Sidebar**: Click the hamburger menu icon
- **Quick Search**: Use the search bar to find anything
- **Breadcrumbs**: Shows your current location, click to go back
- **Notifications**: Click the bell to see alerts and updates

## Your First Steps After Login

### Step 1: Complete Your Profile
1. Click your avatar in the top-right
2. Select "Account Settings"
3. Complete your profile information
4. Upload a profile photo (optional)

### Step 2: Explore the Dashboard
1. Click "Dashboard" in the sidebar
2. View key metrics and statistics
3. Check pending approvals (if any)
4. Review recent activities

### Step 3: Understand Your Permissions
Your role determines what you can do:
- **Read**: View content
- **Write**: Create new items
- **Edit**: Modify existing items
- **Delete**: Remove items
- **Download**: Export data

To check your permissions:
1. Try accessing different modules
2. Modules you can't access won't appear in the sidebar

### Step 4: Set Up Notifications
1. Go to Account Settings → Notifications
2. Configure which notifications you want
3. Choose delivery methods (email, in-app, SMS)
4. Set quiet hours if needed

## Quick Actions from Dashboard

### Creating a New Requirement
1. From Dashboard, click "Create Requirement" button

## Common Questions

**Q: Why can't I see certain modules in the sidebar?**
A: Your role may not have permission to access those modules. Contact your Industry Admin to request access.

**Q: How do I switch between companies?**
A: Log out and log in again, then select a different account at the account selection step.

**Q: Can I customize the dashboard?**
A: The dashboard layout is standardized, but you can filter and sort data within each section.

**Q: How do I change my password?**
A: Go to Account Settings → Security → Change Password.

**Q: Where can I see my activity history?**
A: Dashboard shows recent activity. Detailed audit logs may be available in Settings for admins.
