# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is Diligince.ai?
Diligince.ai is a B2B procurement platform that connects industry buyers with vendors and professionals. It streamlines the entire procurement lifecycle from requirement creation to vendor payment.

### Who can use Diligince.ai?
- **Industry Users (Buyers)**: Companies needing products/services
- **Vendors**: Service providers and suppliers
- **Professionals**: Individual experts and consultants

### What can I do on the platform?
As an Industry user:
- Create procurement requirements
- Receive and compare vendor quotations
- Issue and track purchase orders
- Manage vendor relationships
- Analyze procurement performance

### Is there a mobile app?
The platform is fully responsive and works on mobile browsers. Native mobile apps may be available in future releases.

## Account & Login

### How do I create an account?
Contact Diligince.ai sales or your organization's admin. Industry accounts are created through the registration process.

### I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
1. Go to the login page
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your email
4. Check email for reset link
5. Set new password

### Why can't I log in?
Possible reasons:
- Email not verified (check inbox for verification email)
- Phone not verified (complete phone verification)
- Account suspended (contact your admin)
- Wrong password (try reset)
- Account doesn't exist (check with admin)

### What if I have multiple accounts?
During login, after entering your email, you'll see all accounts linked to that email. Select the one you want to access.

### How do I change my password?
Go to Account Settings → Security → Change Password.

### How do I enable two-factor authentication?
Go to Account Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication → Toggle On.

## Requirements

### How do I create a new requirement?
1. Go to Requirements → Create New
2. Follow the 6-step wizard:
   - Basic Information
   - Requirement Details
   - Documents
   - Approval Workflow
   - Preview
   - Send for Approval

### Can I save my work and continue later?
Yes! Requirements auto-save every 30 seconds after you fill 3+ fields. You can also click "Save Draft" anytime.

### How do I find my saved drafts?
Go to Requirements → Drafts to see all your incomplete requirements.

### Why is my requirement pending?
It's waiting for approval. Check Requirements → Pending Approval to see the approval progress.

### How long does approval take?
Depends on your organization's approval matrix. Each level has a maximum time limit set by admin.

### Can I edit a requirement after submission?
No, once submitted for approval, you can't edit. If changes are needed, ask an approver to reject it, then edit and resubmit.

### What happens if my requirement is rejected?
It returns to you with feedback. Review comments, make changes, and resubmit.

## Quotations

### How do I receive quotations?
Once you publish a requirement, vendors can submit quotations. They appear in Quotations → Pending.

### How many quotations will I get?
Depends on vendor interest. You can also invite specific vendors to quote.

### How do I compare quotations?
1. Select multiple quotations (checkboxes)
2. Click "Compare Selected"
3. View side-by-side comparison

### Can I ask a vendor for more information?
Yes! Use the "Request Clarification" action on any quotation.

### What if I want to approve a quotation but negotiations are needed?
Contact the vendor via Messages. Once terms are agreed, approve the quotation or ask for a revised quote.

### How do I reject a quotation?
Open the quotation, click "Reject", select a reason, add comments, and confirm.

---

## Purchase Orders

### How do I create a purchase order?
1. Go to Quotations → Approved
2. Find the approved quotation
3. Click "Create PO"
4. Review and confirm details
5. Send to vendor

### Can I edit a PO after sending?
Only Draft POs can be edited. Once sent, you may need to cancel and create a new one.

### How do I track PO progress?
Open the PO to see milestones, deliverables, and current status.

### What if a vendor doesn't accept the PO?
Follow up via Messages. You may need to negotiate terms or cancel and find another vendor.

### How do I handle partial deliveries?
Update delivery tracking with partial quantities. Payment can be proportional or wait for completion.

## Team & Roles

### How do I add a team member?
Go to Settings → Team Members → Add Member. Fill in their details and assign a role.

### What roles are available?
Default roles: Industry Admin, Procurement Manager, Finance Manager, Department Viewer. Custom roles can be created.

### How do I change someone's role?
Go to Settings → Team Members, find the person, click Edit, and change the role.

### Can I create custom roles?
Yes, go to Settings → Role Management → Create Role. Define permissions for each module.

### Why can't a team member access certain features?
Their role may not have permission. Check with admin to update role permissions.

## Approvals

### How do approval matrices work?
Approval matrices define who reviews requirements before publishing. They have multiple levels (e.g., Manager → Director → CFO).

### Where do I set up approval matrices?
Settings → Approval Matrix (admin only).

### How do I know if I have pending approvals?
Check Dashboard for pending approval count, or go to the relevant module's Pending section.

### How do I approve something?
Open the item, review details, click "Approve" or "Reject", add comments, and confirm.

## Notifications

### How do I change notification settings?
Go to Account Settings → Notifications. Configure channels, types, and frequency.

### Can I stop email notifications?
Yes, toggle off Email for specific notification types in Account Settings → Notifications.

### What are quiet hours?
Time periods when you won't receive notifications. Set in Account Settings → Notifications.

## Security

### Is my data secure?
Yes, the platform uses encryption for data in transit and at rest. Access is role-based.

### Who can see my information?
Depends on your role and what you share. Company profile is visible to vendors you engage with.

### How do I report suspicious activity?
Contact your admin immediately. Check Active Sessions in Account Settings for unknown logins.
