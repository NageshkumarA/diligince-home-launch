# Diligince HUB Module

## What is Diligince HUB?

Diligince HUB is the vendor and professional discovery marketplace within Diligince.ai. It's where you find, evaluate, and connect with qualified vendors and experts for your procurement needs.

## Key Features

- **Vendor Search**: Find vendors by category, location, capability
- **Profile Browse**: View detailed vendor profiles
- **Verification Status**: See verified vendors
- **Ratings & Reviews**: Community feedback
- **Direct Connect**: Initiate contact with vendors
- **Invite to Quote**: Send requirement to specific vendors

## How to Search for Vendors

### Basic Search
1. Go to Diligince HUB
2. Use the search bar
3. Enter keywords:
   - Company name
   - Service type
   - Product category
4. View results

### Advanced Search
Use filters to narrow results:

**Category**
- Professional Services
- Expert Services
- Products
- Specific sub-categories

**Location**
- City
- State
- Country
- Remote/Global

**Verification**
- Verified only
- All vendors

**Ratings**
- Minimum rating (4+, 3+, etc.)

**Experience**
- Years in business
- Project count

## How to Invite Vendor to Quote

### From Vendor Profile
1. Open vendor profile
2. Click "Invite to Quote"
3. Select the requirement
4. Add personal message (optional)
5. Send invitation

### From Requirement
1. Open your requirement
2. Click "Invite Vendors"
3. Search and select vendors
4. Send invitations
5. Vendors receive notification

---

## How to Contact a Vendor

### Direct Message
1. Open vendor profile
2. Click "Contact" or "Message"
3. Compose your message
4. Send
5. Vendor receives and responds

### Request Meeting
1. Open vendor profile
2. Click "Request Meeting"
3. Choose meeting type:
   - Phone call
   - Video call
   - In-person
4. Propose times
5. Submit request

---

## How to Evaluate Vendors

### Comparison
Compare multiple vendors:
1. Select vendors (checkbox)
2. Click "Compare"
3. Side-by-side comparison shows:
   - Ratings
   - Experience
   - Services
   - Pricing indicators
   - Reviews summary

### Evaluation Criteria
Consider:
- **Experience**: Years in business, relevant projects
- **Ratings**: Overall score, review comments
- **Verification**: Verified status, certifications
- **Capability**: Services match your needs
- **Location**: Proximity, logistics
- **Price**: Competitiveness (if indicated)

## How to Save/Bookmark Vendors

### Adding to Favorites

1. Go to Diligince HUB → My Favorites

## How to Review a Vendor

### After Completing Work
Once you've worked with a vendor:
1. Go to the completed PO
2. Click "Rate & Review"
3. Rate on criteria:
   - Quality of work
   - Communication
   - Timeliness
   - Value for money
   - Overall satisfaction
4. Write review comments
5. Submit

### Review Guidelines
- Be honest and fair
- Provide specific examples
- Keep it professional
- Your review helps others

## Common Questions

**Q: Are all vendors on HUB verified?**
A: Not all. Look for verification badges. Verified vendors have undergone document checks.

**Q: Can I see vendor pricing?**
A: General pricing indicators may be shown. Actual pricing comes through quotations.

**Q: How do I know if a vendor is reliable?**
A: Check ratings, reviews, verification status, and years of experience. Past project count indicates track record.

**Q: Can vendors see my profile?**
A: Vendors see limited info. Your company profile is visible when you invite them or they receive your requirement.

**Q: What if I can't find a suitable vendor?**
A: Publish your requirement. Vendors actively search for opportunities and may find you.

**Q: Can I filter by vendors who've worked with companies like mine?**
A: Some filters may include industry experience. Check vendor portfolios for relevant past work.

**Q: How do I report a problematic vendor?**
A: Contact Diligince.ai support with details. The platform investigates and takes action if needed.
